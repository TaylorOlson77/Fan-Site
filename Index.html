Why I love Rick and Morty

I love Rick and Morty because it is a funny show to watch, the characters have grown a lot in the past three seasons and have shown that the show can convey a more serious side, as well as a comical side. My favorite episode is the first episode, the creators do an awesome job of intorducting you to new characters while being very funny and fast paced.
